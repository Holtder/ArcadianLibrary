	## The Embergrave Manor
**NPCs:**  
- **Lord Hieran Embergrave** (Tiefling) — Gruff, weary, clinging to fading authority.  
- **Mikhael Embergrave** (Tiefling) — Gentle, sickly, fascinated by birds and poetry.  
- **Ferdinant Embergrave** (Tiefling) — Sharp-minded, dutiful, quietly resentful of his father.

**Description:**  
A once-grand brick manor now dulled by coal dust. Its tall windows are always shuttered, giving it a brooding silhouette against the hills. Inside, the halls are lined with portraits of Embergraves past, their eyes seeming to follow visitors with judgmental disdain.

## The Coalspine Station
**NPC:**  
- **Stationmaster Ruldo Vex** (Human) — Nervous, chain-smoking, terrified of disappointing the TRC.

**Description:**  
A long, soot-stained brick building with iron beams and a single platform. The station’s telegraph room clicks day and night, relaying quotas and reprimands from the capital. A massive coal hopper dominates the yard, constantly fed by carts from the mines.

---

## The Black Lantern Inn & Tavern
**NPC:**  
- **Marla “Lantern” Dovekin** (Human) — Sharp-tongued, perceptive, secretly sympathetic to rebels.  
- **Grem Tallowfoot** (Halfling) — Cook; cheerful but always listening.

**Description:**  
A low-ceilinged timber building lit by a single enchanted lantern that never goes out. The air smells of coal smoke, ale, and fried root vegetables. Travelers, miners, and guards mingle uneasily here, making it the village’s social pressure valve.

---

## Tallow & Flint Trading Post
**NPC:**  
- **Berrick Flint** (Dwarf) — Gruff merchant, obsessed with fair deals.  
- **Sera Tallow** (Human) — Friendly, practical, keeps the books and the peace.

**Description:**  
A cluttered shop filled with crates of dried food, tools, lantern oil, and occasional luxuries brought by caravans. The counter is reinforced with iron plates — a relic from a past robbery attempt. A chalkboard lists fluctuating coal prices and TRC requisitions.

---

## The Gully Hill Guardpost
**NPCs:**  
- **Captain Varn Delk** (Human) — Dutiful, humorless, loyal to the Embergraves.  
- **Sister Halvine** (Aasimar) — Local inquisitorial liaison; serene but unsettling.  
- **Jorren Pike** (Human) — Young guard, easily swayed, eager to prove himself.

**Description:**  
A squat stone building beside the tracks, its barred windows glowing with torchlight. The holding cells are cramped and cold, with iron manacles bolted to the walls. A shrine to the God‑Queen stands in the entry hall, its skull sigil polished daily.

---

## The Deepcut Mines
**NPC:**  
- **Foreman Kreg Orlov** (Half-Orc) — Gruff but fair, respected by miners.  
- **Old Nessa** (Human) — Elder miner, rumored to know forbidden stories of the land.

**Description:**  
A sprawling network of tunnels descending into the black earth. Wooden supports creak ominously, and the air is thick with coal dust. Lanterns flicker along the main shaft, but side tunnels vanish into darkness where strange echoes sometimes drift.

---

## The Embergrave Chapel of the God-Queen
**NPC:**  
- **Priestess Veyra Embergrave** (Tiefling) — Cousin to the lord; fervent, charismatic, feared.

**Description:**  
A narrow stone chapel with a towering stained-glass window depicting the God‑Queen descending from the heavens. Incense burns constantly, masking the scent of coal. Villagers attend out of obligation rather than devotion, watched closely by Veyra’s acolytes.

---

## The Sootmarket
**NPC:**  
- **Dallen Reed** (Human) — Market warden, opportunistic, always “taxing” extra.  
- **Various traveling merchants** — Rotate weekly; gossip flows freely here.

**Description:**  
A muddy square lined with makeshift stalls. Merchants sell everything from pickaxes to questionable meat pies. The market is the village’s rumor mill, where whispers of rebellion, shortages, and strange happenings in the mines circulate.

---

## The Old Railworker’s Shack
**NPC:**  
- **“Old Crank” Jebbin** (Gnome) — Retired engineer, paranoid, claims the mines “go deeper than they should.”

**Description:**  
A leaning shack filled with rusted tools, broken lanterns, and half-finished contraptions. Jebbin spends his days tinkering and muttering about “things beneath the rails.” Children dare each other to knock on his door.

---

## The Ashfall Bridge
**NPC:**  
- **No permanent NPCs**, but often patrolled by guards.

**Description:**  
A narrow stone bridge crossing a deep gully filled with blackened water runoff from the mines. The bridge is slick with soot, and the gully below hisses with chemical fumes. Locals avoid it at night, claiming to hear whispers rising from the depths.
