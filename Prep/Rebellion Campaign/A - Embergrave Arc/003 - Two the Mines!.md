# Introduction
Jacob Monaghue, Ravenar Grime, and Thaldin Swiftstrike met by chance on a carriage bound for Tiressea, only for the ride to end violently when it crashed at the edge of Gully Hill. As guards pulled you from the wreck, the trunk revealed the desiccated corpse of young Mikhael Embergrave. Captain Varn Delk and Sister Halvine questioned you, but lacking proof of guilt, Delk offered a choice: clear your names by investigating, or fall into Embergrave service forever.

Your search led through taverns, temples, and back alleys. You learned the Deepcut mines (recently modernized with TRC money) had been shut down after a monstrous infestation, straining House Embergrave. Tensions in Gully Hill were clear when you fought desperate local youths turned thieves. Priestess Veyra explained the political pressure on the Embergraves, and warned of Ferdinant’s ruthless ambition. One that was confirmed when you met him. 

A fire at the church gave you the chance to slip into Lord Hieran’s study, where you discovered the lordling’s fierce love for his brother. With that knowledge you calmed Ferdinant enough to examine Mikhael’s remains, whose wounds pointed unmistakably toward the mines. Speaking with Raskin Drude and 'Old Crank' Jabin confirmed your next destination: the upper levels of the Deepcut mines—and perhaps the Shimmerstone while you’re there.

That was where we left off at the beginning of last session! After some banter with the locals, you went off to the Deepcut mines, where you met Kreg Orlov, the foreman of the mines. He, after sizing you up, let you three in, where you were quickly met with a group of gremlins. It took some quick thinking, quicker casting and the quickest of hand-burning shootin', but you shook them off.
